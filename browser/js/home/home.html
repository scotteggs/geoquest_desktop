Features of GeoQuest

All quests include:

  • Sequential traversing of geographic checkpoints
  • The option of inviting as many friends as you wish from your phone's contact list
  • Real-time updating of other players' locations
  • Live chat with other travelers
  • Live updates of other travelers' progress on the quest

As quest designer, you can:

  • Place checkpoints anywhere you wish simply by dragging and dropping
  • Write messages and choose custom images that travelers see along the way
  • Create location-specific questions that travelers must answer to progress
  • Implement random shuffling of the sequence of checkpoints to accomodate large events

Why Use GeoQuest?

GeoQuest makes designing multi-location activities easy: anything from walking tours, to bar crawls, to large-scale competetive scavenger hunts.

Remember the sense of adventure you got from scavenger hunts when you were a kid? Now that we all have real lives, those kind of activities are few and far between. Geoquest makes organizing them a cinch, and takes care of the operation side of things.
